ORGANONS OF IMPERIAL MEMORY

IMPERIAL INSTITUTIONS

Archive  ·  Vision  ·  Dominion

Beyond the Spheres of open authority stand the institutions that preserve doctrine, interpret omens, and maintain the Empire's deeper machinery of continuity.

Institution I · Custodianship

Sith Curators

Preserve · Classify · Remember

The Sith Curators safeguard the Empire's relics, chronicles, archives, and doctrinal records. They ensure that knowledge is not merely stored, but weaponized through careful classification and controlled access.

Institution Leader: Darth Dimmadome

Mandates

  • Maintain imperial archives, registers, and protected records.
  • Preserve artifacts, texts, and historical evidence of Sith authority.
  • Validate institutional doctrine before it enters official use.
Institution II · Recruitment

The Sith Recruiters

Seek · Vet · Enlist

The Sith Recruiters identify worthy prospects, guide initiates toward the Empire, and uphold standards at the gates of imperial service. Their work strengthens the ranks by ensuring new members enter with discipline, purpose, and loyalty.

Mandates

  • Seek and engage prospective members of the Empire.
  • Vet recruits for discipline, loyalty, and rule compliance.
  • Guide accepted initiates toward the proper imperial pathway.
