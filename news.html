Empire Expands Its Reach

The Sith Empire has successfully established new outposts across previously uncharted sectors of the galaxy, marking a significant expansion of our territorial control. These strategic positions will serve as forward operating bases for future operations and provide crucial supply lines for our military campaigns.

The expansion was coordinated by the Sphere of Military Command, with the Sith Marauders leading the initial assault forces to secure these regions. Their swift and decisive actions ensured minimal resistance and maximum territorial gain.

These new outposts will be garrisoned by elements of the Dark Honor Guard, ensuring the security and loyalty of these vital assets. The Empire's reach grows stronger with each passing cycle, and our dominion extends further into the unknown regions.

New Recruitment Drive Launched

The Emperor has issued a call to all force-sensitives across the galaxy, inviting those who seek power through the Dark Side to join the ranks of the Sith Empire. This comprehensive recruitment initiative aims to identify and recruit promising candidates who demonstrate the potential to serve the Empire's grand vision.

The Sphere of Law and Order, led by the Sith Inquisitors, has been tasked with evaluating potential recruits. Their expertise in identifying force sensitivity and assessing loyalty to the Dark Side ensures that only the most worthy candidates are accepted into our ranks.

Training programs have been expanded across all three spheres, with specialized curricula designed to develop recruits into formidable agents of the Empire. Whether you seek to become a Marauder, join the Honor Guard, or walk the path of the Inquisitor, the Empire offers a place for those with the strength and dedication to serve.

7/4/2026

Victory at the Korriban Front

Sith forces have secured a decisive victory at the ancient Valley of the Dark Lords on Korriban, reclaiming this sacred world for the Empire. The battle was fierce, but our forces prevailed through superior tactics and unwavering commitment to the Dark Side.

The Sith Marauders played a crucial role in this campaign, leading the assault on enemy strongholds and breaking through defensive lines with their characteristic ferocity. Their mastery of lightsaber combat and force abilities proved instrumental in securing key positions.

The Dark Honor Guard provided critical support, protecting our command structures and ensuring the safety of high-value targets throughout the engagement. Their disciplined formations and defensive prowess allowed our offensive forces to operate with confidence.

This victory not only secures Korriban but also provides access to ancient Sith artifacts and knowledge hidden within the valley's tombs. The Sphere of Law and Order has already begun cataloging these discoveries, which will undoubtedly strengthen our understanding of the Dark Side.
