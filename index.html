5000 BBY, Dromund Kaas

▼

A millennium after the fall of the ancient Sith Empire, a new power emerged from the ashes of extinction. Guided by the surviving Dark Lords who escaped during the Great Exodus to Dromund Kaas, the Sith rebuilt themselves in secrecy, hidden beyond the reaches of the known galaxy.

For over a thousand years, the reconstituted Empire remained isolated—reforging its armies, preserving the teachings of the dark side, and preparing for the day of reckoning. On the storm-shrouded world of Dromund Kaas, the Sith forged a civilization built upon absolute power, ruthless ambition, and unwavering obedience to the will of the Emperor.

Now, the time of concealment has ended.

The Sith Empire returns with a singular purpose: the complete domination of the known galaxy.
