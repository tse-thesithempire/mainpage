SITH CURATORS

Preserve  ·  Chronicle  ·  Convene

The Sith Curators are the memory of the Empire. Where warriors carve dominion into the present, the Curators inscribe it into history — archiving the Sith legacy, shaping the art and media that carry its image outward, and gathering the faithful in rites both faction and public.

Notice Concurrent membership in pathway organizations is permitted alongside membership in this institution.
Domains · Authority

Fields of Custodianship

Archive · Image · Assembly

The Curator's authority reaches across three domains — the written record, the imperial image, and the gathering of the faithful. Together they sustain the memory, myth, and community of the Empire.

Domains

  • Historical Record — chronicles, registers, and archives.
  • Imperial Media — art, video, and outward-facing image.
  • Assembly — faction ceremonies and community gatherings.
Leadership · Authority

Institution Leader

Command · Stewardship · Legacy

The Institution Leader oversees the Curator's operations, ensuring the preservation of imperial memory and the execution of the three mandates. They serve as the voice of authority, guiding the archive's direction and representing the Curators before the Dark Council.

Current Leadership

  • Darth Dimmadome — Loremaster and Keeper of Darkness.
Directives · III

Curator Mandates

By Order of the Dark Council

Every Curator swears themselves to three standing mandates. These are the pillars of the office — the same duties expected of every member of the institution, without exception.

Mandates

  • Maintain imperial archives, registers, and protected records.
  • Preserve artifacts, texts, and historical evidence of Sith authority.
  • Study sith history and lore
