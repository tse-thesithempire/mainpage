ABOUT US

Who We Are

Built upon the rich foundations of both Star Wars Legends and Canon, the faction strives to recreate the atmosphere, military structure, traditions, and philosophy of the galaxy with as much authenticity as possible while adapting them to engaging gameplay.

Combat & Progression

Every aspect of the faction is designed to encourage realism, discipline, and meaningful progression. From organized military operations and coordinated formations to rank advancement, specialized divisions, and large-scale campaigns, members participate in an environment where teamwork, leadership, and tactical decision-making are emphasized above all else. Combat is centered around skill, strategy, and coordination rather than individual performance alone, creating battles that are both challenging and rewarding.

Living Lore

Beyond the battlefield, the faction offers a living and evolving setting where lore plays a central role. Events, ceremonies, diplomatic interactions, and story-driven operations are inspired by established Star Wars history while allowing members to actively shape the faction's ongoing narrative. This creates a dynamic experience in which every campaign, victory, and defeat contributes to a broader story within the universe.

Authenticity & Immersion

Drawing extensively from both Star Wars Legends and Canon, the faction incorporates authentic military doctrine, customs, iconography, and organizational structures to preserve the identity of the era it represents. Members are encouraged to immerse themselves fully in their roles, fostering an atmosphere that feels true to the Star Wars universe while remaining accessible to newcomers and veterans alike.

The Experience

Whether participating in intense lightsaber duels, coordinated military offensives, defensive campaigns, specialized training exercises, or narrative events, members become part of a community dedicated to excellence, authenticity, and long-term development. Through a combination of competitive combat, immersive roleplay, structured organization, and faithful world-building, the faction seeks to provide one of the most comprehensive and engaging Star Wars experiences available within Saber Arena.
